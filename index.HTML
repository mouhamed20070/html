RASHFORD'S STORY:

Marcus Rashford is an English footballer who plays as a striker for Manchester United and the England national football team.
Joining Manchester United at the age of seven, he scored two goals on his first-team debut against Midtjylland in
the UEFA Europa League in February 2016 and his Premier League debut against Arsenal three days later.
